My Story

I graduated in 1991 with a BA in chemistry from Connecticut College. Shortly after, I fell in love with the idea of building/repairing guitars. I graduated from Roberto-Venn School of Luthiery in 1992. I happily repaired guitars for 21 years, after which medical issues forced me to stop.

Fortunately, I have always had a keen interest in computer matters of all kinds. I love to figure out what makes them tick, and how how to make them do whatever I need them to do. It was only natural that I became interested in coding, which I have been learning through many resources online such as Codecademy, FreeCodeCamp, Stack Overflow and countless others. I find myself intrigued by html, css, JavaScript, etc. I'm always thrilled when I've learned something new and useful!
